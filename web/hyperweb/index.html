About HyperChain

HyperChain is a next-generation Layer-0 protocol designed for scalability, interoperability, and long-term security. It combines Directed Acyclic Graphs (DAGs) with traditional blockchain mechanisms, enhanced by post-quantum cryptography and on-chain governance.
